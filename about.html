About

Ever been curious about what kind of games you like? The GamExplorer helps you discover the similarities between your favorite video games. This site has been made in an effort to help connect gaming enthusiasts with the sort of interactive experience that excites them. The user can accomplish this through two different methods of displaying the info they search for, a text-based list view and a more visually oriented chart.

This tool was in part inspired by the Guardian article on Gun Violence in top selling games.

powered by the www.giantbomb.com/api, the D3.js, Underscore.js, and jQuery libraries.
